1: Scientific Investigation Skills and Career Exploration

This is an on-going unit that will be assessed in each of the following units. Students will demonstrate scientific investigation skills (related to both inquiry and research) in the four areas of skills (initiating and planning, performing and recording, analysing and interpreting, and communicating). The financial literacy will be studied by identifying and describing careers related to the fields of science under study (e.g. chemists, pharmaceutical chemists, chemical engineering technologists, food scientists) and the education and training necessary for these careers, and describe contributions of scientists, including Canadians, to those fields.
